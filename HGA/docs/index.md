At its core, HGA is built on a **multi-agent architecture**. Instead of one monolithic AI, a team of specialized agents collaborates:

-   The **Context Extractor** understands your business problem.
-   The **Hypothesis Generator** formulates the statistical hypotheses.
-   The **Test Recommender** suggests the right statistical test.
-   The **Statistical Validator** acts as a peer reviewer to ensure quality.
-   The **Reviewer** synthesizes everything into a final, polished report.

This collaborative approach, powered by [CrewAI](https://crewai.com/), ensures a level of depth, accuracy, and reliability that a single AI model cannot achieve.
